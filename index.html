GoCorsicana

GoCrosicana is mobile friendly role-based portal created to mainstream day to day tasks found within the mattress manufacturing industry. The responsive application is compatible with mobile, tablet and desktop devices. The goal of GoCorsicana is to aggregate information in to one location where it can be easily accessed and used.

Intended Users and Problems Addressed

GoCorsicana has been built with the intention of having many types of users. Depending on the user’s role they have access to specific applications and certain privileges within it. One of our most frequent users are sales reps. Sales reps are a significant part of the manufacturing eco system and are responsible for growing Corsicana Mattresses market share.

They accomplish this by understanding their customer’s needs, being knowledgeable of our brands, and offering products at the best price. Before GoCorsicana was created a centralized location for reports and information did not exist. A rep had to access multiple systems to acquire customer information. On top of not being able to easily access data, many processes involving the sales rep weren’t automated.

  • Accessing multiple systems to obtain customer information was insufficient and created confusion amongst the sales reps.
  • Reports weren’t always accurate and the ones available did not provide all the data needed.
  • The lack of automation within common processes consumed unnecessary time from sales reps and other company departments.

GoCorsicana’s purpose is to eliminate unnecessary human interaction, aggregate reports into one centralized location, and simplify day to day task done for sales reps and other company departments.

Achieving the Goal

Understanding weak points, determining priorities and developing solutions are all actionable items necessary to enhance Corsicana Mattresses’ performance. However, none of these are easy tasks and a lot of hours are put into each of them. By investing time in to each of these items we are able to make calculated decisions on which process or item would have the biggest impact once mainstreamed.

My Process

  • Inquire about current processes in specific department and identify any weak points or deficiencies.
  • Understand the context and requirements of a process and adjust it by eliminating redundant or unnecessary steps.
  • Discuss and evaluate possible solutions with the users.
  • Create new process workflow based on solution type and review the new approach with the users.
  • Design high level mock-ups and evaluate designs to determine best user experience.
  • Discuss and evaluate possible solutions with the users.
  • Create detailed mock-ups with interactive workflow.
  • Share interactive mock-ups with users, gather feedback, and adjust mock-up if needed.
  • Work with developers to create user stories for developing solution.
  • Supervise development and answer any behavior or logic questions.
  • Assist with QA and address any bugs found.
  • Oversee release to production and run sanity check.
  • Once released, monitor new application or adjustments for user issues. Work with users to determine future features or adjustments.
