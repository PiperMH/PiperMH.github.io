Project Description

Design and develop a mobile friendly role-based portal to aggregate reports and processes for Corsicana Matttress Company.

Reasoning

Over the past decade more and more companies are migrating and creating a virtual presence. By removing human interaction from different processes companies and customers reap the benefits. Customers can access tracking information with a click of a button, companies can repurpose manpower to other crucial areas of their businesses. Innovative companies like Amazon, and Google have revolutionized the concept of an "Online Company".

By redesigning the way Corsicana Mattress does business the company they will be able to help customer for efficiently, increase market share, build a stronger brand, increase credibility and reputation, and become more cost efficient.

Objective

I designed and helped develop a role based online portal that aggregates reports and business applications in to one easily accessible location.

Challenges

  • Required a complete understanding of the company's infrastructure which included current employee roles and privileges, the structures of the enterprise and transportation management system.
  • Portal architecture must be able to adapt and scale for future requirements.
  • Integrate third party applications

Role

  • User Interface Designer
  • Content Designer
  • Front End Developer

OUTLINING THE PROBLEM

Research

Before creating detailed mock-ups I was interested reviewing other SAAS applications. My goal was to understand what layouts and designs were most commonly used. By comparing other websites I would get insight in to what other companies view as the best user experience.

Insights

After reviewing multiple SAAS applications I was able to gather enough information to determine which design approach would be best for a text heavy SASS. The following are some common themes I found.
  • Compatible with multiple browsers
  • Responsive and mobile friendly
  • Light template themes
  • Collapsible or thin navigation
  • Initial high level dashboard

Implementation

Based on my research, I was able to outline important features necessary to create a productive application.

Accessibility

  • Easy athentication and password manager
  • Intuitive navigation and site map
  • Distraction free
  • Minimal actions
  • Compatibility with different devices and browsers

Content

  • Initial dashboard with KPIS and links to applications
  • Analytic Reports
  • Purposeful Applications
    • Order Tracker
    • Customer Onboarding
    • Customer Catalog

Project Description

Design and develop a mobile friendly role based order tracking application.

Reasoning

Many companies now offer online tracking for customer orders, which has caused it to become an expected service. One of the biggest issues for customers when ordering from manufactuers is not knowing an ETA for your shipment. Without the ETA customers spend unnecesary money on dayworkers to help unload orders. BY building an online order tracker Coriscana Mattress will be able to help their customers.

Objective

I designed and helped develop a role based order tracking application that allows sales managers, sales reps, and customers track orders.

Challenges

  • Determining the best approach to pulling data from EP and TMS systems
  • Designing an easy to read mobile responsive interface that is data and text heavy

  • User Interface Designer
  • Content Designer
  • Front End Developer
